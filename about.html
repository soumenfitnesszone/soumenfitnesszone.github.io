Our Story

Soumen Fitness Zone is located at 4/2k/46, Ho Chi Minh Sarani, Sarsuna Satellite Township, near Sarsuna College in Kolkata, West Bengal, India.It was founded by Soumen with the aim of helping individuals achieve their fitness goals. With modern exercising machines, Yoga Training, Personal Training, Diet and Nutrition Consultation, and discounted Supplements and gym accessories, the Gym offers a comprehensive fitness experience. Online booking and nearby delivery services add to the convenience. Dedicated to inclusivity and personalized attention, the Gym provides a supportive environment for all fitness levels. Join today and experience a transformative fitness journey with top-notch facilities, expert guidance, and convenient amenities.
